Beyond Food: Biogas Processing

Not all food is fit for human consumption. At Replate, we ensure that no food goes to waste. Expired or inedible food is sent to our partner facilities where it is converted into clean, renewable biogas. This process not only prevents landfill waste but also creates a sustainable energy source for communities.
